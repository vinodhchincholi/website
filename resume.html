Fine-tuning Large Language Models

  • Significant amount of time dedicated for preprocessing & encoding to make suitable for analysis. Binary encoding for yes/no features, ordinal encoding for education and one-hot encoding for remaining features.
  • Since our labels were skewed, recall was chosen as evaluation metric to err on catching potential customers.
  • Used grid-search for hyper-parameter tuning and got accuracies > 85%. Adaboost won with 89% accuracy.
